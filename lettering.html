EL CHISMECITO BAR

In these pieces of handrawn lettering and madly illustrated wallpapers you'll find a huge amount of booze, smoke and very sassy humor. The overall goal was to create a crazy and cramped athmosphere where the clients could find many hidden messages around them in every visit. A lot of cultural and pop references as well as local easter eggs were splashed upon those cubicles, in order to keep visitors entertained and curious while enjoying their drinks and cigarrettes.

This inked mayhem was especially designed for "El Chismecito Bar" in Cuba ("The Little Gossip" in Spanish), and it's a cozy little spot that smells like cigars and feels like home...

CAFE BISCUIT

Interior graphics and lettering for "El Biscuit Cafe-Theater", headquarters of "Teatro el Portazo" and quite a hotspot in Matanzas City's (Cuba) night life. These walls were meant to witness some of the country's more controversial and rebellious theater plays, therefore, the general athmosphere and interior vibe should be equally bold and unique. From the bar to the stage the whole space is full of spicy and twisted phrases waiting to be read by curious eyes...

ESA TALLA

"Esa Talla" (meaning 'That Stuff' in Cuban lingo), is a Bar - Gallery originally created for the purpose of combining food and drinks with the permanent exhibition of unconventional Designer Lamps and Luminaires. Since it was going to be placed in an often crowded riverside boulevard, where many other bar-like bussiness existed; to stand out visually was a must. Keeping that in mind, along with the peculiar 'drinks-lights' combo and adding some 'fisherman's tavern' flavour, I opted for a graphic strategy that reflected that concept in a creative and attractive way. Using a lettering composition and vibrant colors to represent a "bottled light" resulted in an accurate and eye catching solution...
