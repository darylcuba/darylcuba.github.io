ILLUSTRATING BOOKS IS ALWAYS AN INTERESTING TASK BUT ALSO DEMANDS A LOT OF RISPONSABILITY, ESPECIALLY WHEN WORKING FOR KIDS AND TEENS, BECAUSE IT IS QUITE POSSIBLE THAT YOUR REPRESENTATION WILL BE THE FIRST REFERENCE TO THOSE STORIES THAT THEY'LL EVER SEE. BEING ABLE TO SHOW MY PERSONAL TAKE ON SOME CLASSICS LIKE POE OR CARPENTIER HAS BEEN BOTH AN HONOR AND A PLEASURE.

THE WASP'S NEST

These vibrant images constitute a sample of some interior illustrations I had the pleasure of making for "The Wasp's Nest", featuring Agatha Christie's famous Hercule Poirot, and many other classic detective short stories by authors like Edgar Allan Poe and Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. All of them were nicely compiled in a single volume by Editorial Andarele...

THE KINGDOM

No colors were left behind while portraying the magic in "The Kingdom Of This World" , by Alejo Carpentier. This famous novel is packed with all the mysticism and charming of afro-caribbean culture and folklore. This particular edition was meant for educational purposes at highschool level so i was able to use a dynamic and somehow toony visual language...

ALAS A LA DERIVA

Of these comic flavoured scenes is filled "Alas A La Deriva", a nice compilation of amazonian jungle stories by Horacio Quiroga. For this teen focused edition I opted for an 'inked' graphic vibe, in a high contrast black and white scheme with dynamic compositions to add movement and generate visual interest...
