SOCIAL MEDIA

Social media is crucial for digital marketing due to several key reasons, providing an unparalleled opportunity to reach a large and diverse audience. Consistent and strategic social media presence helps increase brand visibility and awareness...

Creating a design that works seamlessly across different social media platforms involves the understanding of the unique requirements and best practices of each platform while maintaining a consistent brand identity. Since each platform has specific image size requirements, it's important to create responsive designs that are adaptable to different screen sizes and orientations (mobile, tablet, desktop).

It is by carefully considering each platform's specifications and maintaining a cohesive and consistent tone, how I can create designs that look great and perform well across all the best known social media channels...





Cubatel has always been very active in Social Media, and not only because of the obvious use as a promotional platform, but also as an effective way of keeping contact with clients and followers. During COVID 19, for example, making good use of the Company's online reach; many health focused messages were displayed through illustrated posts and carousels...
