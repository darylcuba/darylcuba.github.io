NYHR FILM FEST

In the conceptualization process of the "NEW YORK HUMAN RIGHTS FILM FESTIVAL" visual identity, I decided to combine strong and constrasting graphic codes with the undeniable sensitivity of such a touching event. Working through that concept resulted in the conception of the 'HEARTED FILM' symbol, which powerfully illustrates the idea of 'pouring the heart into the celluloid', and successfully functioned as this 5TH Edition's official face...

THE LONELY ROAD

"The Lonely Road" (ORLANDO FILM FESTIVAL 2017 Official selection) is a simply animated video clip for a song about faith and the inner journey of a lifetime by American musician Derek Gazal, AKA Blindman. Though following a specific script, I did have the chance to bring some of my personal visual flavour to this cute little piece...

GLOVES & PAPERS

"Gloves without ring" (DOC NYC 2023 Official selection) and "No Papers" are two social themed documentary films by young cuban filmmaker Karen Sotolongo, they reach deep into important issues of modern society like discrimination, racism and emigration.

We decided to use the main characters image in each case to give it a more personal tratment since these are real life stories where they are the actual protagonists. Similar graphic codes and vibe were used in both cases trying to visually unify them as part of a larger series, although each film is an independent piece on its own...
