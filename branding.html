AINO

AINO is a conscious Canadian cosmeceutical company, dedicated to the crafting of organic beauty and healthcare products that respect the enviroment. Geometrical and light shapes were used in the making og this visual identitity in order to evoke elegance and cleanness. The chromatic range was defined with sobriety, keeping the visuals simple and solid; and also a minimalistic approach was preferred for it's packaging design...

MATIZ

MATIZ is a restoration group formed by young Cuban plastic artists, who make sure to put all of their artistic talent and skills in total function of the remodeling, recovery and decoration of any type of space. The 'brush-tip-shaped' capital 'M' symbol and color palette were concieved through the simple concept: 'where construction meets art'...

PEQUEÑO SUAREZ

"PEQUEÑO SUAREZ" (Spanish for 'Little Suarez') Is a family owned restaurant in Varadero Beach. Since there was an already well known Suarez Restaurant in town (owned by another family member), this new endeavour needed to stand out by itself and not as a smaller version or branch of the other, in spite of the shared name. Therefore, a new image was concieved, creatively using the name nnitial letters to smoothly represent a smoking-hot-ready to serve dish, delivered with a strong contrast color palette that says: 'We're here, We are different and We mean Bussiness...
