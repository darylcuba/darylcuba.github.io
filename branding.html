PEQUEÑO SUAREZ

PEQUEÑO SUAREZ (SPANISH FOR 'LITTLE SUAREZ') IS A FAMILY OWNED RESTAURANT IN VARADERO BEACH. SINCE THERE WAS AN ALREADY WELL KNOWN SUAREZ RESTAURANT IN TOWN (OWNED BY ANOTHER FAMILY MEMBER), THIS NEW ENDEAVOUR NEEDED TO STAND OUT BY ITSELF AND NOT AS A SMALLER VERSION OF THE OTHER, IN SPITE OF THE NAME. THEREFORE, A NEW IMAGE WAS CONCIEVED, CREATIVELY USING THE NAME INITIALS TO REPRESENT A SMOKING-HOT-READY TO SERVE DISH, DELIVERED WITH A STRONG CONTRAST COLOR PALETTE THAT SAYS: WE'RE HERE, WE ARE DIFFERENT AND WE MEAN BUSSINESS...
