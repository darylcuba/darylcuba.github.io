FESTIVAL INTERNACIONAL DE TEATRO CALLEJERO IX

FOR A FEW CONSECUTIVE YEARS I HAD THE PLEASURE OF DESIGNING THE GRAPHIC IMAGE AND MAKING THE TV SPOTS OF “EL CALLEJERO”, WHICH IS WHAT THE INTERNATIONAL STREET THEATER FESTIVAL OF MATANZAS, IN CUBA, IS LOVINGLY KNOWN. IT IS A VERY RENOWNED EVENT IN THE COUNTRY AND IN OTHER PLACES OF THE WORLD WHERE STREET THEATER IS MADE AND RESPECTED. CONCEIVING THE VISUALS OF THE SAME FESTIVAL IN DIFFERENT EDITIONS WAS VERY INTERESTING FROM A CREATIVE POINT OF VIEW SINCE IT FORCED ME TO LOOK FOR DIFFERENT GRAPHIC SOLUTIONS TO THE SAME TOPIC...

CALLEJERO X

FOR THE 10TH EDITION OF THE FESTIVAL I WENT FOR A MORE URBAN FLAVOUR, USING WORDPLAY AND VIBRANT COLORS TO ENGAGE AN OLDER AUDIENCE, SINCE THE FEST ITSELF HAD GROWN AND EVOLVED WITH SOME PLAYS ALSO SUITABLE FOR MORE MATURE PUBLIC...

CALLEJERO XII

THE POST-PANDEMIC EDITION WAS OF COURSE SMALLER AND SIMPLER BUT IT REALLY MEANT A LOT FOR ACTORS AND PERFORMERS THAT FINALLY COULD BE BACK ON THE STREETS. ONCE AGAIN I GOT THE CHANCE TO CREATE THE FESTIVAL'S GRAPHICS, AND THIS TIME I TRIED TO REPRESENT THAT MIX OF CURFEW SADNESS WITH THE NEW OUTDOOR JOY...
